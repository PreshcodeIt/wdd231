Hair by Pretty Rukky - Website Site Plan

Site Name

Name: Hair by Pretty Rukky

This name reflects the personal brand and professional identity of a passionate hairstylist and wig expert. It brings a personal touch and helps build trust with potential clients.

Optional domain: hairbyprettyrukky.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to promote Hair by Pretty Rukky's wig and hairstyling services. Visitors can explore available wigs, view style galleries, get hair care tips, book appointments, and contact the stylist. It will also include testimonials, FAQs, and links to social media pages.

Scenarios

  • How do I book an appointment for hairstyling or a wig fitting?
  • Can I order a custom wig and how long does it take?

Color Schema

These are the selected colors and where they will be used:

  • #9b4dca – Main accent color (used in headings, buttons, highlights)
  • #fdd9c7 – Background color and cards/sections

Typography

  • Playfair Display – For main headings to create a classy and elegant look.
  • Roboto – For paragraph and body text to ensure readability and modern styling.
